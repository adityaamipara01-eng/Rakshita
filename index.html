How it works

Emergency Response Lifecycle

A secure, multi-layered shield that coordinates community and law enforcement instantly.

01

One-Tap Activation

Tap the big SOS button. A 3-second cancellable countdown begins to prevent false alarms, emitting cautionary tones.

02

Instant Dispatch

Coordinates are transmitted instantly to the Police Control Center, triggering silent recordings and alerts to contacts.

03

Active Intervention

Responders track your location in real-time, dispatching the nearest vehicle to intercept and resolve the threat.

Comprehensive Shield

Advanced Safety Features

More than just a distress button. A complete set of tools to prevent, defend, and document.

Panic SOS Siren

Triggers flashing sirens and continuous alarm audios. Plays Web Audio synthesizers if the hardware audio fails.

Safe Haven Routing

Plots local safe zones like police booths, metro desks, and hospitals, calculating the safest pathways to reach them.

Check-In Timer

Walking alone? Set a timer. If you don't check in before it expires, the system automatically fires an SOS alert.

Fake Call Simulator

Simulate an incoming phone call with ringing graphics and options to help exit uncomfortable circumstances safely.

Threat Incident Log

Log harassment or physical threats anonymously. Coordinates are logged to build hot-spot warning maps.

Hybrid Backend

Runs offline in mockup database mode, and upgrades to real-time Supabase cloud sync instantly with your keys.
